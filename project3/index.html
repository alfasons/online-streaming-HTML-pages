"NAIVE VILLAGE GIRL, DESPERATE COUPLE AND A GANGSTER ..." What could go wrong?

MEROESTREM & NUBIAN PRODUCTIONS

OVERVIEW

Leo brings his village girlfriend to Lagos, with the promise of success. Problem is that Leo is a gambler and loser who always finds a way to lose money. After making money from surrogacy, which Leo gambles and squanders away, the girlfriend has had enough. She escapes Leo to do the runs on her own, but Leo is not easily shaken off. The wealthy couple she is carrying the pregnancy for are keen to protect their unbron child which brings them into confrontation with Leo. Ad they accommodate the surrogate for her protection, they did not realise they were about to activate a bomb that cannot be defused until it goes off.
